Hamzeen is a Technical Lead based in The Hague. He believes, architecture is the foundation for building robust and scalable applications, and is a strong advocate of clean code and thoughtful user experience.

Some of his specializations include System Design, SOLID Principles, Design Patterns, RESTful web services, Responsive Design, Micro Frontends, Serverless Architecture, Cloud Computing & DevOps.

After leading software teams for 7 years, he is now passionate about building high-performance teams and driving efficiency in software delivery through agile methodologies.

Hamzeen is a graduate of University of Westminster, London. He holds a first class Bsc(Hons) degree in Software Engineering (2010).
